      • LDL-C > 190 mg/dL
        Start high-intensity statin therapy.
      • Treatment Guidelines
        Age Patient characteristics ASCVD risk or LDL levels Recommendation
        ASCVDTreat with high intensity statins or maximum tolerated dose with goal of reducing LDL-C.
        Very high risk ASCVDConsider adding non-statins in addition to statins therapy
        Age 40-57 Diabetic
        		If LDL-C > 70-189: Start moderate-intensity statin therapy.

        For primary ASCVD prevention, have risk discussion with patient before starting statin therapy.
        Non-Diabetic If ASCVD risk > 7.5%:Start moderate-intensity statin if discussion of treatment options favors statin therapy
        If ASCVD risk 7.5-19.9% (intermediate):Favor starting statin therapy if risk-enhancing factors present*
        If LDL > 70-189: Consider measuring CAC if uncertain whether to begin statin therapy.
